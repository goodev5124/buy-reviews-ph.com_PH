A fake Google review is a comment posted on a Google Business listing that does not come from a genuine customer who used the service or product. In practice, the line is blurry: many businesses actively ask their satisfied customers to leave a review, which is already a form of "prompted" review. Buying fake Google reviews simply takes this approach further by using professional writers.
