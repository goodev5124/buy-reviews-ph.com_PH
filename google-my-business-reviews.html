What is Google My Business and why does it matter?

Google My Business (renamed Google Business Profile) is Google's free tool that lets businesses manage their online presence. Your GMB listing displays your opening hours, address, photos and, above all, your customer reviews. It is the first thing consumers see when searching for a local business. Buying Google My Business reviews turns this storefront into a conversion machine.

How reviews influence the ranking of your GMB listing

Google's algorithm takes into account three factors for local ranking: relevance, distance and prominence. Google My Business reviews are the most powerful prominence signal. Google favours listings with a large number of recent reviews, a high rating and owner responses. Every positive review strengthens your position in the local pack.

The Google local pack: how reviews propel you into it

The local pack (the 3 results shown with the Google Maps map) captures more than 44% of clicks for local searches. To appear there, your Google My Business listing must have a rating above 4 stars and a significant number of reviews. Without reviews, you have no chance of appearing against better-rated competitors.
