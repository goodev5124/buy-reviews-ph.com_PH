How to calculate the number of reviews to raise your Google rating

Improving your Google rating is a matter of mathematics. The displayed rating is a weighted average of all your reviews. If you currently have 20 reviews with a 3.0 rating, you need about 40 five-star reviews to reach 4.3. With 50 existing reviews at 3.5, you will need about 50 new ones to reach 4.2.
